Discover, Support, Inspire

Colville Chamber of Commerce is a growing organization that offers incredible benefits to its members, such as trainings, social events, advertising and more. By joining, you too can have access to these benefits. See below for the list of member benefits.






Membership Level Benefits

  • Gold:
    - Two tickets to our annual Chamber Luncheon.
    - Subscription to our Monthly newsletter.
    - Three advertisements on our Chamber website.
    - Three Printed Advertisements in The Spokesman Review.
  • Silver:
    - Two tickets to our annual Chamber Luncheon.
    - Subscription to our Monthly newsletter.
    - Three advertisements on our Chamber website.
  • Bronze:
    - Subscription to our Monthly newsletter.
    - Three advertisements on our Chamber website.
  • Non-Profit:
    - Subscription to our Monthly newsletter.
