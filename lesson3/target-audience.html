Target Audience

The target audience is for those native to or currently living in south eastern Idaho, more specifically the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The age range of our target audience is between 12 and 84.

Two Personas:

Jared Young

Job Title: Dentist
Age: 25
Education: College Graduate
Ethnicity: White
Family Status: Unmarried
His Goal in Using the Site: Jared frequently frequently uses this site for hiking information such as weather and trail conditions. Occasionally he will use the site for event information when he is planning a date.
Physical, Social and Technological Environment: He is very physically active during his off time, and enjoys meeting up with friends who also enjoy outdoor activities. He is comfortable using technology, but does not spend a lot of leisure time on it.

Tamara Barnes

Job Title: Elementary School Teacher
Age: 34
Education: College Graduate
Ethnicity: African American
Family Status: Married with Children
Her Goal in Using the Site: Tamara uses this site for planning family activities while also being an active part in community events.
Physical, Social and Technological Environment: She enjoys running on the weekends, is involved in several community events, and is familiar with the use of technology to the extent of its use within the classroom.

Scenarios:

  • Jared is planning a day trip on the weekend to the mountains and wants to know what the weather will be like.
  • Tamara has heard about a farmers market but doesn't know the details like when and where.
  • Jared is looking for an event to take his date to.
  • Tamara wants to know the weather for her students in Preston.
  • Jared came across a hiking trail blocked by a fallen tree and wants to report it for other hikers.
  • Tamara's class is doing a fundraiser and they want to involve the community.
