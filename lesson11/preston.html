COVID-19 Updates

Many of you are asking about the COVID-19 vaccine, and when that will be available in our area. Franklin Medical Center has provided us some information.
Healthcare Personnel & Long-term care facility Residents began recieving vaccinations in December of 2020. Then in January-February of 2021, frontline workers and adults 65 years and older were given the opportunity to recieve a vaccine. Coming this month, March, through April 2021 other essential workers and those with high risk medical conditions will be offered the vaccine.
By May of 2021, we are anticipating vaccines being made available to the general public.
If you have any further questions the COVID-19 hot-line at Willow Valley is (208) 840-1146
